AKAN NAMES

A web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week
they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their akan Names.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are
given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week
they were born.

FEMALE

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

MALE

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
